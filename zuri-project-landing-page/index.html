Frequently Asked Questions

What Is MetaData?

Metadata, is basically data that provides information about other data. It is used to summarize basic information about data that can make tracking and working with specific data easier.

What are the benefits of extracting your metadata on PROTO?

You get to save data extracted on PROTO and can come back to download it. PROTO allows you to share different file formats on your social media platforms.

Which files can I extract on PROTO?

You can extract jpeg, png, pdf, csv, json.

How can I download or save my metadata on PROTO?

By clicking a button and it automatically downloads, also you can always save your files, documents or images on our platform and come back to download them.

Does PROTO have the database for my files?

Yes, PROTO stores your uploaded files in a database so that you can always redownload them. However, you are free to delete these files at will.

How long can I embed my uploaded files on the website?

The files stays forever.
